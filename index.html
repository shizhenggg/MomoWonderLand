How to play

There are two objectives to this game:
1. Escape without meeting MoMo
2. Try to collect evidence on the way
Pick up items along the way to help with your escape. However, some routes are only possible if you choose to certain items

Who is MoMo?

MoMo is a humanoid bird monster that gained notoriety through the viral MoMo challenge in 2018. She will be hunting you at every step of your escape so avoid her at all cost

WARNING:
 There will be jumpscares in this game generated randomly
